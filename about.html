About Me

Behind e-estate is a vision to empower tea growers through knowledge. I’m Nirmana Fernando, an undergraduate student at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, passionate about revolutionizing the agricultural sector. Through this app, I aim to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern agricultural practices, helping cultivators make informed decisions for better productivity. Join me in this journey towards a smarter, more sustainable future for tea farming!
